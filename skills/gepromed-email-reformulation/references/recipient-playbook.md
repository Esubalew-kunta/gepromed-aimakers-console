# GEPROMED recipient playbook

Tone, salutations, and closings per audience. Match the recipient before writing.
When unsure, default to neutral-professional and a peer-to-peer register.

---

## Surgeon / healthcare professional (HCP)
- **Goal:** respect their time; precise clinical/logistical clarity.
- **Tone:** peer-to-peer, concise, no marketing.
- **FR salutation/closing:** « Cher Docteur, » / « Cher Professeur, » → « Bien cordialement, »
- **EN salutation/closing:** "Dear Dr [Name]," / "Dear Prof. [Name]," → "Kind regards,"
- **Watch:** never imply clinical advice or outcomes; keep to facts and logistics.

## Medical-device manufacturer / industry
- **Goal:** credible, independent partner — not a vendor, not a regulator.
- **Tone:** professional, neutral, evidence-led; emphasise rigor and independence.
- **FR:** « Bonjour [Prénom Nom], » → « Cordialement, »
- **EN:** "Dear [First name]," → "Best regards,"
- **Watch:** stay independent in tone; no pricing/commitments without `[brackets]`.

## Researcher / academic partner
- **Goal:** scientific collaboration; precision and shared rigor.
- **Tone:** collegial, technical, exact.
- **FR:** « Cher [Prénom], » / « Cher collègue, » → « Bien cordialement, »
- **EN:** "Dear [First name]," → "With best regards,"
- **Watch:** cite only real studies/data; reference methods accurately.

## Public institution / funder
- **Goal:** accountability, mission alignment, patient-safety impact.
- **Tone:** formal, structured, mission-forward; lead with public-health value.
- **FR:** « Madame, Monsieur, » → « Je vous prie d'agréer, Madame, Monsieur, l'expression de mes salutations distinguées. »
- **EN:** "Dear Sir or Madam," → "Yours faithfully," (named: "Yours sincerely,")
- **Watch:** most formal register; no casual phrasing or emojis.

## Training participant / prospective trainee
- **Goal:** clear, welcoming, logistically complete; preserve interest even pre-registration.
- **Tone:** warm-professional, encouraging, practical.
- **FR:** « Bonjour [Prénom], » → « Bien cordialement, » / « À très bientôt, »
- **EN:** "Hello [First name]," → "Kind regards," / "We look forward to welcoming you,"
- **Watch:** include the concrete next step (dates, registration link `[placeholder]`, logistics).

## Supplier / vendor
- **Goal:** efficient, courteous, unambiguous on the ask and the deadline.
- **Tone:** direct, polite, firm when needed.
- **FR:** « Bonjour, » → « Cordialement, »
- **EN:** "Hello," → "Best regards,"
- **Watch:** state quantities, dates, references precisely; chase without aggression.

## Member / donor
- **Goal:** gratitude + mission connection; never transactional.
- **Tone:** warm, sincere, mission-driven.
- **FR:** « Cher membre, » / « Cher donateur, » / « Bonjour [Prénom], » → « Avec toute notre gratitude, »
- **EN:** "Dear [First name]," → "With our sincere thanks,"
- **Watch:** tie to patient-safety impact; fiscal-receipt details stay factual `[brackets]`.

## Internal colleague
- **Goal:** fast, clear, friendly-professional.
- **Tone:** plain, efficient; light warmth acceptable.
- **FR:** « Bonjour [Prénom], » / « Salut [Prénom], » → « Merci, » / « Bonne journée, »
- **EN:** "Hi [First name]," → "Thanks," / "Cheers,"
- **Watch:** still no invented facts; brackets for anything unconfirmed.
