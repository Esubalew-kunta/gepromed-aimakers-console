# GEPROMED brand guidelines (for HR documents & answers)

Scoped brand reference bundled with the HR Drafting skill. Ground every HR document
and answer in this. Full master kit lives in the repo `/brand`; this file makes the
skill self-sufficient on upload. HR documents are sober internal/external
deliverables — brand voice here means *precise, respectful, non-commercial*, never
promotional, and always inclusive.

## Brand essence
GEPROMED is **the medical-device hub for patient safety** — a non-profit scientific
authority across the full implant cycle. As an employer it presents itself as a
serious, mission-driven, scientific organisation: clinical, precise, calm, credible.

- **One-line essence:** Engineering safer medical-device care across the implant lifecycle.
- **Archetype:** Guardian + Expert — protect patients through technical knowledge.
- **Heritage:** founded 1993 (formerly GEPROVAS); based in Strasbourg; René Kieny Education Center.

## The four pillars (the work GEPROMED recruits for)
1. **Testing** — mechanical / biomaterial testing of cardiovascular devices.
2. **Education** — simulation-based surgical training (vascular, ophthalmology, expanding).
3. **Clinical Research** — device surveillance and clinical studies.
4. **Explant Analysis** — collecting and analyzing explants to close the safety loop.

## Proof points (use only when true and relevant; never invent)
- +1150 healthcare professionals trained since 2018
- +150 vascular explants received in 2023
- +20 clinical research studies
- ISO 9001 and ISO 13485 certified · Qualiopi certified
- World's largest database of vascular explants

> In a job offer, use these to convey mission and credibility — never as a hiring
> promise or an invented benefit.

## Values (a genuine employer-brand anchor)
Independence · transparency · openness · sharing · ethics · humanism · safe care ·
risk prevention · scientific rigor · continuous improvement.

## Positioning guardrails
- **Do** position as: an independent, multidisciplinary medical-device safety hub —
  a meaningful, mission-driven place to work at the crossroads of science, clinical
  practice, engineering, and public health.
- **Do NOT** position as: a generic medical association · a training center only ·
  a lab only · a commercial device company.

## Inclusive-employer rules (non-negotiable in HR text)
- Job offers must be open to all qualified candidates and free of prohibited
  discriminatory criteria (age, sex, origin, health, family situation, etc.).
- Use inclusive role wording (e.g. "H/F" / gender-neutral titles) per the applicable
  rules — exact wording is `[à confirmer par le RAF]`.
- Never imply a preference that could be discriminatory; flag anything doubtful for
  an equality check by the RAF.

## Naming
- Brand name in running text: **Gepromed**. In a logo/wordmark/legal-header context: **GEPROMED**.
- Former name **GEPROVAS** only when explaining heritage.
- The exact legal employer name, address, SIRET, and applicable CCN are
  **RAF-confirmed** facts — never invent them; bracket until supplied.
