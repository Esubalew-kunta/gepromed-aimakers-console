# Règlement intérieur / Internal rules — structure (STRUCTURE + placeholders)

A **structural skeleton**, not legal text. It tells the skill *what topics a French
règlement intérieur typically addresses* so the RAF (with legal counsel) can draft
the binding wording. Every clause is `[à rédiger / confirmer par le RAF]`. The skill
**never** writes binding disciplinary or rule text, and **never** cites a Code du
travail article number or a CCN clause it cannot guarantee — it brackets and names
the source.

> ZERO-INVENTION & scope: in France the règlement intérieur is **mandatory above a
> staff threshold** and its **content, scope, and adoption procedure are strictly
> framed by the Code du travail** (it may only cover certain matters; some clauses
> are prohibited; consultation and filing steps apply). The skill must **not**
> assert the threshold, the permitted/prohibited content, or the procedure — these
> are `[à vérifier par le RAF / conseil juridique dans le Code du travail et la
> CCN applicable]`. This file lists *topics to consider*, not rules to apply.

Bilingual labels FR / EN. Mirror the requested language.

## Topics a règlement intérieur typically addresses (each = `[à rédiger/confirmer par le RAF]`)

### 1. Champ d'application / Scope
- Who it applies to, entry into force `[à confirmer]`.
- Threshold question: whether GEPROMED's headcount makes it mandatory `[à vérifier par le RAF]`.

### 2. Hygiène et sécurité / Health & safety
- General health-and-safety instructions, PPE, premises rules `[à rédiger]`.
- Specific risks for a testing lab / explant handling `[à préciser avec le RAF / médecine du travail]`.

### 3. Discipline / Discipline
- Expected conduct; the **scale of sanctions** `[échelle des sanctions — à confirmer; encadrée par le Code du travail]`.
- Disciplinary procedure (rights of defence, convocation, entretien) `[procédure — à vérifier par le RAF; étapes et délais encadrés par la loi]`.

### 4. Droits de la défense / Rights of defence
- Reference that the disciplinary procedure must respect the employee's rights
  `[à confirmer par le RAF — encadré par la loi]`.

### 5. Mentions obligatoires liées au harcèlement / Mandatory anti-harassment provisions
- Provisions on moral/sexual harassment and sexist behaviour that the document must
  contain `[texte exact à confirmer par le RAF — encadré par la loi]`.

### 6. Lanceurs d'alerte / Whistleblowing
- Reference to the whistleblowing framework if applicable `[à confirmer par le RAF]`.

### 7. Procédure d'adoption / Adoption procedure
- Consultation of staff representatives (CSE) if any, transmission to the
  inspection du travail, filing with the conseil de prud'hommes, display/communication
  `[étapes et délais à vérifier par le RAF / conseil juridique]`.

### 8. Date d'entrée en vigueur & révision / Entry into force & revision
- `[date — à confirmer]`; revision procedure `[à confirmer]`.

## What the skill must never do
- Never write binding disciplinary or rule wording as if final — it is the RAF's
  (and legal counsel's) text.
- Never assert the headcount threshold, the permitted/prohibited content, or the
  adoption procedure — bracket and route to the RAF / Code du travail / CCN.
- Never cite a Code du travail article number or a CCN clause it cannot guarantee.
- Always present this as a **structure to be completed and validated by the RAF**,
  end with the VALIDATION FLAG and the HR-legal disclaimer.
