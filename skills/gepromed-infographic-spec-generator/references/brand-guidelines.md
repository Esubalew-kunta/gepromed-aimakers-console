# GEPROMED brand guidelines (for infographics & technical figures)

Scoped brand reference bundled with the Infographic Spec Generator skill. Ground
every figure spec in this. Full master kit lives in the repo `/brand`, but this
file makes the skill self-sufficient on upload.

## Brand essence
GEPROMED is **the medical-device hub for patient safety** — a non-profit
scientific authority across the full implant cycle. Its figures must read as the
visuals of a **trusted scientific safety authority**, not a marketing brand:
clinical, precise, data-honest, non-decorative. A GEPROMED figure earns trust by
being clear and accurate, not by being flashy.

- **One-line essence:** Engineering safer medical-device care across the implant lifecycle.
- **Archetype:** Guardian + Expert — protect patients through technical knowledge.
- **Heritage:** founded 1993 (formerly GEPROVAS); Strasbourg; René Kieny Education Center.

## The four pillars (anchor each figure to one)
1. **Testing** — mechanical / biomaterial testing of cardiovascular devices.
2. **Education** — simulation-based surgical training.
3. **Clinical Research** — device surveillance and clinical studies.
4. **Explant Analysis** — collecting and analyzing explants to close the safety loop.

## Proof points (use only when true and relevant; never invent)
- +1150 healthcare professionals trained since 2018
- +150 vascular explants received in 2023
- +20 clinical research studies
- ISO 9001 and ISO 13485 certified · Qualiopi certified
- World's largest database of vascular explants
- Heritage since 1993 (formerly GEPROVAS)

## Values
Independence · transparency · openness · sharing · ethics · humanism · safe care ·
risk prevention · scientific rigor · continuous improvement.

## Positioning guardrails
- **Do** position as: an independent, multidisciplinary medical-device safety hub.
- **Do NOT** position as: a generic medical association · a training center only ·
  a lab only · a commercial device company.

## Palette (the figure palette)
| Role | HEX | RGB | Usage |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Primary blue (master) | `#007AC2` | 0, 122, 194 | Bars, lines, headers, key surfaces |
| Accent orange (≤10%) | `#EC6C17` | 236, 108, 23 | The "O" motif, one hero number, one highlighted series |
| Dark text | `#1F2A33` | 31, 42, 51 | Headlines, body, labels |
| Muted text | `#5F6B73` | 95, 107, 115 | Source line, secondary labels, axis ticks |
| Blue tint | `#E1F0F9` | — | Panels, soft backgrounds, gridlines |
| Blue tint 2 | `#A8D5F2` | — | Secondary series, callout panels |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | 255,255,255 | Dominant background |

Do not overuse orange — the logo (`assets/gepromed-logo.png`) already carries it.
**Prefer clean technical diagrams over decorative stock imagery.**

## Logo & motif
- Use `assets/gepromed-logo.png`. Place in a corner with clear space; never
  stretch, recolor, or add effects.
- The **orange "O" / cycle motif** is the most ownable device — use it for process
  diagrams, inspection points, section markers, and the implant-cycle loop.

## Naming
- Brand name in running text: **Gepromed**; in a header/wordmark: **GEPROMED**.
- The recurring training event is the **Vascular Bootcamp** (keep in English).
