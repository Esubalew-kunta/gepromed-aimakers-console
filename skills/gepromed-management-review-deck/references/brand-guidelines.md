# GEPROMED brand guidelines (for the management-review deck & minutes)

Scoped brand reference bundled with the Management Review Deck skill. Ground the
deck styling and the written narrative in this. Full master kit lives in the repo
`/brand`; this file makes the skill self-sufficient on upload. The deck is an
internal quality deliverable — brand voice here means *precise, calm, evidence-led*,
never promotional.

## Brand essence
GEPROMED is **the medical-device hub for patient safety** — a non-profit
scientific authority across the full implant cycle. In writing it must feel like
a **trusted scientific safety authority**: clinical, precise, calm, credible,
non-commercial.

- **One-line essence:** Engineering safer medical-device care across the implant lifecycle.
- **Archetype:** Guardian + Expert — protect patients through technical knowledge.
- **Heritage:** founded 1993 (formerly GEPROVAS); based in Strasbourg; René Kieny Education Center.

## The four pillars (often the QMS processes reviewed)
1. **Testing** — mechanical / biomaterial testing of cardiovascular devices.
2. **Education** — simulation-based surgical training (vascular, ophthalmology, expanding).
3. **Clinical Research** — device surveillance and clinical studies.
4. **Explant Analysis** — collecting and analyzing explants to close the safety loop.

## Proof points (context only; never use as invented KPI values)
- +1150 healthcare professionals trained since 2018
- +150 vascular explants received in 2023
- +20 clinical research studies
- ISO 9001 and ISO 13485 certified · Qualiopi certified
- World's largest database of vascular explants

> These are brand proof points, **not** the current review-period figures. Never
> drop them into a KPI slide as if they were this period's results — those come
> from the RQ's source documents and are bracketed until supplied.

## Values
Independence · transparency · openness · sharing · ethics · humanism · safe care ·
risk prevention · scientific rigor · continuous improvement.

## Positioning guardrails
- **Do** position as: an independent, multidisciplinary medical-device safety hub.
- **Do NOT** position as: a generic medical association · a training center only ·
  a lab only · a commercial device company.

## Palette (deck styling)

|---|---|---|---|
| Primary blue | `#007AC2` | 0, 122, 194 | Title bars, headings, dividers, primary accent |
| Accent orange | `#EC6C17` | 236, 108, 23 | Rare section markers / highlights (≤10%) |
| Dark text | `#1F2A33` | 31, 42, 51 | Body text, slide titles |
| Muted text | `#5F6B73` | 95, 107, 115 | Footers, captions, secondary text |

Do not overuse orange — the logo (`assets/gepromed-logo.png`) already carries it.
Keep slides clean and white-spaced; the generator applies this automatically.

## Naming

- Former name **GEPROVAS** only when explaining heritage.
- The exact QMS scope, certificate numbers, and process names are **RQ-confirmed**
