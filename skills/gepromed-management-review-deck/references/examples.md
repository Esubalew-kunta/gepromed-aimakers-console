# Management Review Deck & Minutes — worked examples

These show the target quality and the **discipline**: §9.3 structure, GEPROMED
house voice, and — above all — **every figure/result/decision either supplied or
bracketed for the RQ**, never invented. The deck is generated by
`scripts/generate_review_deck.py`; the minutes are written directly.

---

## 1 — FR · Deck mode (FULL), user gives the period only, then replies `go`

**Request:** "Prépare la revue de direction 2025 (ISO 9001 §9.3) en .pptx." → after
the FULL intake, user replies `go`.

**Response shape:**
```
Mode: Deck (FULL)
Intake tier: FULL — go: building the standard §9.3 deck with figures bracketed.

Deck outline (16 slides):
 1. Page de titre — "Revue de direction — Année 2025" (logo, statut PROJET)
 2. Ordre du jour
 3. Contexte & périmètre du SMQ — [périmètre — à confirmer par le RQ]
 4. Suivi des actions des revues précédentes — [statut par action — à confirmer]
 5. Évolutions internes & externes — [à compléter]
 6. Satisfaction client & parties intéressées — [score — à confirmer]
 7. Atteinte des objectifs qualité — [statut par objectif — à confirmer]
 8. Performance des processus — [KPI par processus — à confirmer]
 9. Non-conformités & actions correctives — [nombre, nature, statut — à confirmer]
10. Résultats d'audit — [constats / écarts — à confirmer]
11. Prestataires externes — [performance — à confirmer]
12. Ressources, risques & opportunités — [à confirmer]
13. Opportunités d'amélioration — [à compléter]
14. Objectifs qualité — revus / nouveaux — [à confirmer]
15. Décisions & plan d'actions — table avec [responsables] et [échéances] bracketés
16. Clôture & validation — VALIDATION FLAG (RQ)

Generated: revue_direction_2025.pptx  (open and review; every figure is a
bracketed placeholder coloured in orange so the RQ can spot it)

Bracketed items for the RQ to confirm:
- [périmètre du SMQ], [satisfaction], [objectifs], [KPI processus], [NC],
  [audit], [prestataires], [ressources], [décisions], [actions/owners/deadlines]

⚠️ VALIDATION FLAG — RQ : projet de revue de direction. Chaque KPI, résultat
d'audit, non-conformité, score de satisfaction et décision doit être validé par le
Responsable Qualité avant présentation.

QA: 97/100
```
Generation command used:
```bash
python scripts/generate_review_deck.py --demo --out revue_direction_2025.pptx
# (or build content.json from the user's supplied figures and use --content)
```
**Moves:** the deck is complete in *structure* but invents **no** figures — every
KPI is bracketed and orange-flagged in the slides; RQ validation is explicit.

## 2 — EN · Deck mode (FULL), user supplies some real figures

**Request (after intake):** "Build the §9.3 deck for 2024. Training satisfaction
was 4.6/5. Audit: 2 minor non-conformities, both closed. Everything else, leave for
the RQ."

The skill builds a `content.json` using **only** the supplied figures and brackets
the rest:
```json
{
  "title": "Management Review — 2024",
  "period": "2024",
  "sections": [
    {"heading": "Customer satisfaction & interested parties",
     "bullets": ["Training satisfaction: 4.6/5 (source: [survey ref — RQ to confirm])",
                 "Qualitative feedback: [to confirm]"]},
    {"heading": "Audit results",
     "bullets": ["Internal audit: 2 minor non-conformities, both reported closed",
                 "Findings detail: [to confirm by the RQ]"]},
    {"heading": "Process performance", "bullets": ["[KPIs per process — to confirm by the RQ]"]}
  ],
  "decisions": ["[to confirm by the RQ]"],
  "actions": [{"action": "[to define]", "owner": "[to assign]", "deadline": "[to define]", "status": "[to do]"}],
  "validator_role": "RQ"
}
```
**Moves:** supplied numbers (4.6/5, "2 minor NCs closed") are placed verbatim with
their source still to confirm; **nothing else is invented**; the rest is bracketed.

## 3 — FR · Minutes mode (MINIMAL), from supplied notes

**Request:** "Rédige le compte rendu de la revue de direction à partir de ces
notes : présents Dr Martin (Direction), Mme Roux (RQ). Discuté satisfaction
formations (bonne), audit interne (1 NC sur la traçabilité). Décidé : revoir la
procédure de traçabilité. Action confiée à Mme Roux."

**Draft:**
```
Mode: Minutes (MINIMAL)
Intake tier: MINIMAL — writing from your notes.

Revue de direction — Compte rendu (PROJET)
Période revue : [période — à confirmer]   ·   Date : [date — à confirmer]
Rédacteur : [nom — à confirmer]   ·   Statut : projet, à valider par le RQ

Participants
- Présents : Dr Martin (Direction), Mme Roux (RQ)

Points discutés
- Satisfaction des formations : retour qualifié de « bonne » (score chiffré :
  [à confirmer par le RQ]).
- Audit interne : 1 non-conformité relevée sur la traçabilité.

Décisions
1. Revoir la procédure de traçabilité.

Plan d'actions
| # | Action | Responsable | Échéance | Statut |
|---|--------|-------------|----------|--------|
| 1 | Réviser la procédure de traçabilité | Mme Roux (RQ) | [échéance — à définir] | À faire |

Conclusion
Prochaine revue : [date — à confirmer].

⚠️ VALIDATION FLAG — RQ : projet de compte rendu, à relire et valider par le
Responsable Qualité avant diffusion. Chiffres, décisions et actions à confirmer.

QA: 96/100
```
**Moves:** uses only what the notes say; the satisfaction *score* is bracketed
(notes only said "bonne"); the deadline is bracketed; no invented attendees.

## 4 — EN · Minutes mode, ambiguous owner handled correctly

**Request:** "Minutes from these notes: decided to launch a supplier reassessment;
action owner not yet named."

**Excerpt:**
```
Decisions
1. Launch a supplier reassessment.

Action plan
| # | Action | Owner | Deadline | Status |
|---|--------|-------|----------|--------|
| 1 | Launch supplier reassessment | [owner — to assign] | [deadline — to define] | [to do] |

⚠️ VALIDATION FLAG — RQ: draft minutes — the RQ assigns the owner and deadline and
validates before circulation.
```
**Moves:** missing owner/deadline are bracketed, never invented; routed to the RQ.

## Anti-pattern gallery (never produce these)
- Putting a brand proof point (e.g. "+1150 trained") on a KPI slide as if it were
  this period's result.
- Inventing a satisfaction score, an NC count, or an audit finding.
- Writing a decision the notes don't contain, or an owner/deadline not supplied.
- Quoting the ISO 9001 standard's exact normative text or inventing sub-clause numbers.
- Returning a deck or minutes with **no VALIDATION FLAG** and no RQ routing.
- An "all green / everything is fine" narrative instead of bracketed placeholders.
