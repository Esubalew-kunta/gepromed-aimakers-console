# Management-review minutes / compte rendu — structure (minutes mode)

In **minutes mode (Intake tier: MINIMAL)** the skill turns supplied meeting notes
into a clean **compte rendu / procès-verbal** of the revue de direction. It
**structures and clarifies** the notes — it never invents attendees, decisions,
figures, owners, or deadlines. Anything missing is `[bracketed]` for the RQ.

> The compte rendu is **documented information** that ISO 9001 §9.3 expects the
> organisation to retain as evidence of the management review. The RQ validates it
> before it becomes the official record.

## Structure (mirror the requested language)

### 1. En-tête / Header
- **Titre :** Revue de direction — Compte rendu / Management Review — Minutes
- **Période revue / Period reviewed:** `[période]`
- **Date & lieu de la réunion / Date & place:** `[date]` · `[lieu / visio]`
- **Rédacteur / Recorded by:** `[nom]`
- **Version / statut:** Projet — à valider par le RQ

### 2. Participants / Attendees
- **Présents / Present:** `[noms + rôles]` (Direction, RQ, pilotes de processus…)
- **Excusés / Apologies:** `[noms]`
> List only the attendees the notes mention. Do not invent names or roles.

### 3. Ordre du jour / Agenda
The §9.3 sections actually covered (see `references/iso-9001-9.3.md`). `[liste]`.

### 4. Points discutés / Points discussed
One short paragraph or bullet per agenda item, summarising what the notes say.
Keep figures exactly as supplied; bracket any figure that is referenced but not
given (e.g. "satisfaction: [score — à confirmer]").

### 5. Décisions / Decisions
A numbered list of decisions taken. Each decision states *what was decided*, in the
words of the notes — never an inferred or invented decision. If the notes are
ambiguous, write `[décision à confirmer par le RQ]`.

### 6. Plan d'actions / Action plan (table)
The key output. One row per action:

| # | Action | Responsable / Owner | Échéance / Deadline | Statut / Status |
|---|--------|---------------------|---------------------|-----------------|
| 1 | `[action]` | `[owner]` | `[deadline]` | `[à faire / en cours / fait]` |

> Never invent an owner or a deadline. If the notes don't give one, bracket it:
> `[responsable — à attribuer]`, `[échéance — à définir]`.

### 7. Conclusion & prochaine revue / Conclusion & next review
- Brief closing statement (from the notes). `[date de la prochaine revue — à confirmer]`.

### 8. VALIDATION FLAG
End every minutes draft with:
> ⚠️ VALIDATION FLAG — RQ : projet de compte rendu, à relire et valider par le
> Responsable Qualité avant diffusion. Les chiffres, décisions et actions doivent
> être confirmés.

## Rules for minutes mode
- Structure and tidy; **do not add** content the notes do not contain.
- Preserve every supplied figure verbatim; bracket every missing one.
- Every action has an owner and a deadline, or a bracket where one is missing.
- Keep it factual and neutral — no editorialising, no optimistic spin.
- Always end with the VALIDATION FLAG naming the RQ.
