# GEPROMED ICPs — ideal customer/partner profiles + value angles

Who GEPROMED reaches out to, what they care about, and **which pillar / proof
point to lead with for each**. Classify the target first, then pick the angle that
matters *to them* — not the angle that is easiest for us to talk about.

GEPROMED's four pillars: **Testing** · **Education** · **Clinical Research** ·
**Explant Analysis**. GEPROMED is **independent and non-profit** — at the crossroads
of academia, clinicians, engineering, industry, and public health. Never sound like
a vendor.

---

## ICP 1 — Medical-device manufacturers / industrials
- **Who:** R&D, quality/regulatory (RA/QA), clinical affairs, post-market
  surveillance teams at cardiovascular (and adjacent) device makers; from start-ups
  to large OEMs.
- **What they care about:** independent evidence, regulatory pressure (MDR,
  post-market surveillance), mechanical/biomaterial test data, real-world device
  performance, explant findings, time-to-evidence, credibility with notified bodies.
- **Lead with:** **Testing** (independent mechanical/biomaterial testing under
  ISO 9001 / ISO 13485) and **Explant Analysis** (the world's largest vascular
  explant database — real post-market signal). Frame as *independent third-party
  evidence*, never as a sales service.
- **Avoid:** sounding like a commercial test-house competing on price; over-claiming
  regulatory conclusions; implying endorsement of their device.
- **Typical objective:** test-platform client · explant-analysis collaboration ·
  post-market surveillance partnership.

## ICP 2 — Surgeons & healthcare professionals (HCPs)
- **Who:** vascular surgeons, ophthalmologists, interventional clinicians, and
  trainees; department heads; KOLs.
- **What they care about:** patient safety, hands-on simulation training, peer
  credibility, contributing to real device knowledge, time efficiency.
- **Lead with:** **Education** (simulation-based surgical training at the René Kieny
  Education Center; Qualiopi-certified) and **Clinical Research / Explant Analysis**
  (a chance to contribute cases and co-author real-world evidence).
- **Avoid:** any marketing tone, any implication of clinical advice, wasting their
  time. Peer-to-peer register only.
- **Typical objective:** training participant/faculty · research/explant contributor.

## ICP 3 — Researchers / academic partners
- **Who:** PIs and labs in biomaterials, biomechanics, cardiovascular engineering,
  public health; academic-medical centres; consortia.
- **What they care about:** scientific rigor, shared methods, data access,
  co-publication, access to a unique explant database and testing capability,
  grant-fundable collaborations.
- **Lead with:** **Clinical Research** and **Testing** capability + the **explant
  database** as a shared research asset. Frame as collegial collaboration.
- **Avoid:** self-praise; cite only real capability and data; reference their work
  accurately.
- **Typical objective:** research collaboration · joint study/grant · data sharing.

## ICP 4 — Institutions, funders & public health
- **Who:** public health bodies, hospital networks, foundations, regional/EU
  funders, regulators-adjacent organizations.
- **What they care about:** patient-safety impact, mission alignment,
  accountability, training reach, independence and non-profit status, measurable
  outcomes.
- **Lead with:** the **mission** (patient safety across the implant cycle) and
  **proof points** (+1150 professionals trained since 2018; +20 clinical studies;
  ISO 9001 / ISO 13485 / Qualiopi). Formal, accountability-forward.
- **Avoid:** commercial framing; vague enthusiasm; unbacked figures.
- **Typical objective:** funding/grant · institutional partnership · training programme.

## Value-angle picker (one line)
| If the target is… | Lead with | One-line angle |
|---|---|---|
| Device manufacturer (R&D/QA) | Testing + Explant Analysis | "Independent third-party evidence for your post-market and design work." |
| Surgeon / HCP | Education + Research | "Hands-on simulation training, and a way to turn your cases into real-world evidence." |
| Researcher / lab | Clinical Research + Testing | "A shared explant database and testing capability for a rigorous joint study." |
| Institution / funder | Mission + proof points | "Independent, non-profit patient-safety impact across the implant cycle." |

## Proof points (use only when true and relevant; never invent)
- +1150 healthcare professionals trained since 2018
- +150 vascular explants received in 2023
- +20 clinical research studies
- ISO 9001, ISO 13485, and Qualiopi certified
- World's largest database of vascular explants
- Independent, non-profit; founded 1993 (formerly GEPROVAS); Strasbourg.

## Independence guardrail (applies to every ICP)
GEPROMED sits *between* clinicians and industry. Outreach must read as an
independent scientific partner offering useful evidence/collaboration — **never** as
a vendor chasing a sale. If a message could come verbatim from a commercial
supplier, rewrite it.
