# GEPROMED outreach — QA rubric (score before returning)

Self-score every draft against this 100-point rubric. **Do not return a draft that
scores below 95.** If below 95, fix the failing criteria and re-score. Keep the
final score in the `QA:` line of the output.

| # | Criterion | Pts | Fail = 0 unless… |
|---|---|---:|---|
| 1 | **Genuine personalization (GATE)** — one real, specific hook tied to the target; no fabricated hook | 18 | The hook traces to supplied target info or is `[bracketed]`; never invented |
| 2 | **Reads human, not automated (GATE)** — no template tells, no merge-field feel, varied rhythm | 15 | Passes the anti-automation checklist; survives the "blast test" |
| 3 | **Zero invented facts** — about the target or GEPROMED (numbers, certs, history, referrals) | 12 | All specifics trace to source or `[brackets]` |
| 4 | **Leads with the target** — relevance first, GEPROMED's offer second, framed as useful to them | 10 | Opens on the target, not a pitch |
| 5 | **One low-friction ask** — single, easy, non-pushy | 8 | Exactly one soft ask; no calendar link in cold first contact |
| 6 | **Independent, non-vendor tone** — partner between clinicians and industry, not a supplier | 10 | No sales/vendor framing |
| 7 | **Right ICP angle** — pillar/proof point matched to the target per `icp.md` | 8 | Correct lead angle |
| 8 | **Channel discipline** — length/shape correct for LinkedIn note / DM / email | 6 | Within channel norms (note ≤~300 chars) |
| 9 | **House voice** — calm, evidence-led, no hype/superlatives/emojis | 6 | Passes voice check |
| 10 | **Right language** — FR/EN matches input or request; native quality | 4 | Output language correct |
| 11 | **Memory applied** — all `MEMORY.md` rules honored | 3 | No stored rule violated |

**Scoring guidance**
- Criteria **1 and 2 are gates**: either failing = the draft is not shippable
  regardless of total. Fix and re-score.
- A fabricated personalization hook is an automatic fail — bracket instead.
- Apply the **blast test**: if the exact message could go unchanged to 500 people,
  criterion 1 fails.
- Round honestly. The goal is a genuinely human, reply-worthy message, not a
  flattering number.
- When you revise after a low score, briefly note internally what you fixed; do not
  expose the iteration beyond the final `QA:` line.
