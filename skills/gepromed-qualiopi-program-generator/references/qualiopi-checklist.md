# Qualiopi training-program requirements (domain reference)

Ground every generated program in this file. It encodes what the **Référentiel
National Qualité (RNQ / Qualiopi)** expects to see in a published training
program ("programme de formation" / "fiche programme") for a certified
organisme de formation (OF). GEPROMED is **Qualiopi certified**; this skill
produces the program document, **not** the certification itself. A human (the
Responsable Qualité — RQ) validates before publication.

> This is regulatory-adjacent content. Never invent indicateurs, certificate
> numbers, prices, dates, or accessibility commitments. Unknown values go in
> `[crochets]` for the RQ to confirm.

---

## 1. What a Qualiopi program document must contain

The RNQ does not impose a single template, but its indicateurs require that the
**information delivered to the public before enrolment** be complete, precise,
and accessible. A compliant GEPROMED program covers the blocks below. Each block
maps to one or more RNQ indicateurs (Critère 1 = information to the public,
Critère 2 = design of the action).

| # | Block (FR) | Block (EN) | Why it is required |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Intitulé de la formation | Course title | Clear, non-misleading designation (Ind. 1) |
| 2 | Public visé | Target audience | Who the action is for (Ind. 1, 4) |
| 3 | Prérequis | Prerequisites | Conditions to follow the action; "aucun" if none (Ind. 1, 4) |
| 4 | Objectifs pédagogiques | Learning objectives | Operational, measurable, action-verb based (Ind. 1, 4, 5) |
| 5 | Contenu / programme détaillé | Detailed content / outline | Sequence of modules / sessions (Ind. 1, 5) |
| 6 | Durée | Duration | Total hours + breakdown; dates or session cadence (Ind. 1, 5) |
| 7 | Modalités pédagogiques | Delivery methods | Présentiel / distanciel / mixte / simulation (Ind. 1, 5, 17, 19) |
| 8 | Moyens techniques et encadrement | Resources & trainers | Equipment, platforms, trainer profiles (Ind. 17, 21, 22) |
| 9 | Modalités d'évaluation | Assessment methods | How acquisition is verified (Ind. 1, 8, 11) |
| 10 | Sanction / validation | Certificate / proof | Attestation, certificat, attestation de fin (Ind. 1, 8) |
| 11 | Accessibilité handicap | Accessibility (disability) | Référent handicap + adaptation process (Ind. 1, 26) |
| 12 | Délais d'accès | Access lead time | Time between request and start (Ind. 1, 4) |
| 13 | Tarifs | Pricing | Price + conditions; financing routes (Ind. 1, 24) |
| 14 | Modalités et délais d'inscription | Enrolment process | How and by when to register (Ind. 1, 4) |
| 15 | Contact / référent pédagogique | Contact / pedagogical referent | Named role to reach (Ind. 1) |
| 16 | Indicateurs de résultats | Results indicators | Satisfaction / success rates (Ind. 2) — only if known |

Blocks 1–14 are always rendered. 8, 10, 15, 16 are rendered when data exists,
otherwise bracketed.

## 2. Writing each block to standard

### Intitulé (1)
Short, descriptive, non-promotional. No superlatives. Example:
"Bootcamp Vasculaire — abord et anastomose sur simulateur".

### Public visé (2)
Name the profession and level precisely. Example: "Chirurgiens vasculaires,
internes en chirurgie vasculaire (à partir de la 3e année), praticiens en
formation continue." Avoid "tout public" for a specialised clinical action.

### Prérequis (3)
State concrete conditions or "Aucun prérequis." For clinical training, this is
usually a professional status, not academic level. Never leave blank — write
"Aucun prérequis" if truly none.

### Objectifs pédagogiques (4)
Use **operational action verbs** (réaliser, identifier, appliquer, évaluer,
maîtriser, analyser). Each objective must be observable/assessable. **Avoid**
"comprendre", "connaître", "sensibiliser" alone — they are not measurable. 3–6
objectives is typical. Each objective should be checkable by the assessment in
block 9.
- Good: "Réaliser une anastomose termino-latérale sur simulateur dans le temps imparti."
- Weak: "Comprendre les anastomoses." (not measurable)

### Contenu / programme détaillé (5)
A sequenced outline: modules or half-days, each with its focus. Tie content back
to the objectives. Use a clean ordered or sectioned list. For simulation, name
the practical workshops.

### Durée (6)
Total in **hours** (the RNQ reasons in hours), plus the calendar shape
("2 jours — 14 heures", "4 demi-journées"). If dates are unknown, write the
cadence and bracket the dates.

### Modalités pédagogiques (7)
Pick from and combine: **présentiel**, **distanciel** (synchrone/asynchrone),
**mixte/blended**, **simulation** (the GEPROMED signature — René Kieny Education
Center). Describe the pedagogy: exposés, ateliers pratiques, mises en situation,
dry-lab / wet-lab, débriefing. For distanciel, name the platform and the
synchronous/asynchronous split (Ind. 19).

### Moyens techniques et encadrement (8 — resources)
Rooms, simulators, consumables, e-learning platform; trainer profiles (clinician
+ expertise). Do not name individuals unless supplied; use role/profile.

### Modalités d'évaluation (9)
State **how acquisition is measured**: pré/post-test, évaluation pratique sur
simulateur via grille, QCM, mise en situation, évaluation par le formateur. Also
state the **satisfaction** evaluation (questionnaire à chaud / à froid). Evaluation
must align with the objectives (4). This is a Qualiopi gate.

### Sanction / validation (10)
What the participant receives: "Attestation de fin de formation" (mandatory),
optionally "Certificat de réalisation", or a specific certificat if the action
leads to one. Never claim a diploma/certification GEPROMED does not deliver.

### Accessibilité handicap (11)
Always present. Standard GEPROMED wording: name a **référent handicap** (role),
state that situations de handicap are studied case by case to adapt the action,
and give the contact route. Never promise a specific adaptation in the generic
program — describe the **process**.

### Délais d'accès (12)
Time between the enrolment request and the session start (e.g. "Inscription
possible jusqu'à [N] jours avant la session, selon les places disponibles").

### Tarifs (13)
Price HT/TTC + what it includes; financing routes (OPCO, prise en charge
employeur, financement personnel). GEPROMED is a non-profit — keep it factual,
not promotional. Bracket any figure not supplied.

### Modalités et délais d'inscription (14)
How to register (formulaire, email, contact) and the deadline.

### Contact / référent pédagogique (15)
A named role (Responsable pédagogique, coordinateur) + contact route.

### Indicateurs de résultats (16)
Satisfaction rate, completion rate, success rate — **only if real figures are
supplied**. Otherwise omit or bracket. Never fabricate.

## 3. GEPROMED-specific defaults (safe to apply, RQ confirms)

- **Sanction par défaut:** "Attestation de fin de formation" + "Certificat de
  réalisation" pour les actions concernées.
- **Accessibilité handicap (texte standard):** voir `intake-questions.md` default
  and the script's built-in paragraph — process-based, names a référent handicap
  role, contact bracketed.
- **Modalité signature:** simulation au René Kieny Education Center (Strasbourg).
- **Certifications mentionnables (vraies):** Qualiopi, ISO 9001, ISO 13485.
- **Évaluation pratique:** grille d'évaluation sur simulateur quand l'action est
  une formation gestuelle.
- **Langue:** FR par défaut pour les programmes destinés au public français ;
  version EN pour les actions internationales.

## 4. Anti-patterns (never produce)

- Objectives starting with "comprendre / connaître / sensibiliser" only.
- "Tout public" / "aucun public défini" for a specialised clinical action.
- A blank Prérequis (use "Aucun prérequis").
- A missing Accessibilité handicap or Évaluation block.
- Invented satisfaction/success rates, prices, certificate numbers, or dates.
- Marketing superlatives ("la meilleure formation", "leader") in the intitulé.
- Promising a diploma or regulated certification GEPROMED does not issue.
