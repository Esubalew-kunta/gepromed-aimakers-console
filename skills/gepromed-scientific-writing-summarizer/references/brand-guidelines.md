# GEPROMED brand guidelines (for scientific writing & summarization)

Scoped brand reference bundled with the Scientific Writing & Summarizer skill.
Ground every summary and drafted section in this. Full master kit lives in the
repo `/brand`, but this file makes the skill self-sufficient on upload.

## Brand essence
GEPROMED is **the medical-device hub for patient safety** — a non-profit
scientific authority across the full implant cycle. In scientific writing it must
read as a **trusted, independent scientific author**: precise, measured,
evidence-led, appropriately hedged, non-promotional. Scientific text is the one
place where GEPROMED's credibility is most directly tested — accuracy and
integrity outrank everything.

- **One-line essence:** Engineering safer medical-device care across the implant lifecycle.
- **Archetype:** Guardian + Expert — protect patients through technical knowledge.
- **Heritage:** founded 1993 (formerly GEPROVAS); based in Strasbourg.

## The four pillars (context for the science)
1. **Testing** — mechanical / biomaterial testing of cardiovascular devices.
2. **Education** — simulation-based surgical training.
3. **Clinical Research** — device surveillance and clinical studies. ← this skill's home
4. **Explant Analysis** — collecting and analyzing explants to close the safety loop.

GEPROMED's research strength is real-world device evidence: explant analysis,
mechanical/biomaterial testing, and device surveillance feeding clinical studies.

## Proof points (context only; never paste a proof point into a manuscript as data)
- +20 clinical research studies · world's largest database of vascular explants
- ISO 9001 / ISO 13485 certified
> Marketing proof points are **not** study data. Never let a brand proof point
> become a result, a sample size, or a citation in a scientific text.

## Scientific-integrity guardrails (the heart of this skill)
- **Never fabricate** data, results, statistics, p-values, sample sizes, effect
  sizes, findings, quotes, or **citations/references** (author, year, journal, DOI).
- **No citation automation** — every reference is human-verified; mark slots
  `[CITATION NEEDED]`.
- **Faithfulness in summaries** — report only what the source states, with its own
  hedges and limitations; add nothing.
- **Within-data in drafts** — claims stay within the supplied findings; hedge
  appropriately; never over-generalize.
- **Independence** — GEPROMED sits between clinicians and industry; writing is
  neutral, not promotional, and avoids unsupported clinical/regulatory conclusions.
