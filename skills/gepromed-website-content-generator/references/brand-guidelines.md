# GEPROMED brand guidelines (for website content)

Scoped brand reference bundled with the Website Content Generator skill. Ground
every page in this. Full master kit lives in the repo `/brand`, but this file
makes the skill self-sufficient on upload.

## Brand essence
GEPROMED is **the medical-device hub for patient safety** — a non-profit
scientific authority across the full implant cycle. On the website it must feel
like a **trusted scientific safety authority**, not a healthcare marketing brand:
clinical, precise, calm, credible, non-commercial — and, critically for web copy,
**clear and proof-led** (the known weakness is copy that reads translated and
weakens authority).

- **One-line essence:** Engineering safer medical-device care across the implant lifecycle.
- **Archetype:** Guardian + Expert — protect patients through technical knowledge.
- **Heritage:** founded 1993 (formerly GEPROVAS); based in Strasbourg; René Kieny Education Center.

## The four pillars (anchor every page to one)
1. **Testing** — mechanical / biomaterial testing of cardiovascular devices.
2. **Education** — simulation-based surgical training (vascular, ophthalmology, expanding).
3. **Clinical Research** — device surveillance and clinical studies.
4. **Explant Analysis** — collecting and analyzing explants to close the safety loop.

## Proof points (use only when true and relevant; never invent)
- +1150 healthcare professionals trained since 2018
- +150 vascular explants received in 2023
- +20 clinical research studies
- ISO 9001 and ISO 13485 certified · Qualiopi certified
- World's largest database of vascular explants
- Heritage since 1993 (formerly GEPROVAS)

> On a given page, use only the proof points that are **true for that page** and
> that the writer has confirmed. Any new figure, partner, or claim goes in
> `[brackets]` for verification — never publish an unverified number.

## Values
Independence · transparency · openness · sharing · ethics · humanism · safe care ·
risk prevention · scientific rigor · continuous improvement.

## Audiences (web)
Surgeons / HCPs · medical-device manufacturers · researchers · public
institutions · funders & donors · training participants · academic partners ·
prospective trainees searching for a course.

## Positioning guardrails
- **Do** position as: an independent, multidisciplinary medical-device safety hub
  at the crossroads of academia, clinicians, engineering, industry, public health.
- **Do NOT** position as: a generic medical association · a training center only ·
  a lab only · a commercial device company.

## Palette (for any visual element / Ibexa styling note)
| Role | HEX | RGB | Usage |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Primary blue | `#007AC2` | 0, 122, 194 | Headings, links, primary accents, dividers |
| Accent orange | `#EC6C17` | 236, 108, 23 | Rare safety/innovation accent (≤10%), the "O"/cycle motif |
| Dark text | `#1F2A33` | 31, 42, 51 | Body text |
| Muted text | `#5F6B73` | 95, 107, 115 | Captions / secondary text |

Do not overuse orange — the logo (`assets/gepromed-logo.png`) and the orange-"O"
cycle motif already carry it. Prefer technical diagrams over stock imagery.

## Naming
- Brand name in running text: **Gepromed**. In a logo/wordmark/header context: **GEPROMED**.
- Former name **GEPROVAS** only when explaining heritage.
- "René Kieny Education Center" — keep this exact English name on every language version.
